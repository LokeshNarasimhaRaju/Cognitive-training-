Dual N-Back Training

  • Auditory Monitoring: Match the spoken alphabet to the one heard N-steps back.
  • Real-time Response: Press buttons as soon as you detect a match.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Use [A] for Position and [L] for Sound.
